JOB DESCRIPTION

I have worked as a tutor at TeachMe2, specializing in modules such as CAD, software development, physics, economics, and mathematics. My experience spans a diverse range of students, including special needs learners, young children, high school students, and even employed adults looking to expand their skill sets.

Through this, I have developed a flexible and adaptive teaching approach, ensuring that each student receives personalized guidance tailored to their learning style. My role has not only strengthened my subject matter expertise but also enhanced my communication, mentorship, and problem-solving skills in an educational setting.



WHY DID I CHOOSE IT?

Beyond academics, tutoring has strengthened my ability to mentor, communicate, and solve problems creatively. It’s fulfilling to help students build confidence in their abilities, whether it’s mastering mathematics, physics, or programming.

The opportunity to make a real difference in someone’s learning journey is what makes tutoring such a passion for me.

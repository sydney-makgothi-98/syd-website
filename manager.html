JOB DESCRIPTION

I worked at City Foods, a food shop in Maseru, initially taking on a temporary role to implement internal control measures aimed at reducing theft within the store. However, after a few months, I was promoted to manager, where my responsibilities expanded significantly.

In this role, I established basic bookkeeping procedures to help the store track its income more efficiently. Additionally, I handled day-to-day customer and employee disputes, ensuring smooth operations and fostering a professional work environment. My time at City Foods strengthened my problem-solving, leadership, and financial management skills in a fast-paced retail setting.
